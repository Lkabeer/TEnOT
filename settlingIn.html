Overview

Job opportunities sometimes come with a price. One perfect example of such a situation is whereby you have been promoted, but the only position available is based in another country, hence you are required to move to this new country. It is this kind of occurrences that make TE&OT work towards ensuring that you, as well as your family members have had an easier move. We understand how sometimes moving can be hectic to people especially when moving to places they have never been before. Our family services entail settling in assistance to our clients through provision of self-stuffiest packages that are normally tailored to make sure that they have catered to every single need of our client. Through working with our well trained professionals, you will be able to get settling-in services that match every single expectation you might bear as far as being assisted into settling into your new residence is concerned. Our goal is to always offer you the easiest transition from your old routines that you had been accustomed to, to being able to get in tune with the new kind of lifestyle that awaits you in your new assignment. We guarantee that your better opportunity is celebrated without you being lost in your new environment.

Nothing beats a good welcoming spirit especially when you are very new to a place that you would be expected to spend a considerable part of your life. This has always been our strategy in making each one of our client feel right at home in their new places of assignments. We also ensure that you have been assisted in all of your airport entry requirements that is covered within our personalized Meet and Greet services. Butterfly (spouses) Community

Job Search Assistance
When your job demands that you relocate, this sometimes means that even your spouse is faced with the challenge to relocate. Through our Butterfly spouses Community program, we are able to offer some services that would help them get better chances of being employed. This includes; career counseling services, prepping them for job interviews, offering them networking contacts and qualified job leads and finally helping them gather market research such as company profiles, professional associations, and fulfill their certification requirements.

Escort Interpretation
TSometimes being a spouse to a working expat could be frustrating especially if you are not lucky enough to land a job as soon as you get to your host country. Being jobless and in a new country could lead to immense boredom which could even lead to a strained marriage. It is therefore by considering such situations that we have been able to offer an Escort Interpreter to expat spouses. The role of these very well trained professionals is to help the spouses be able to navigate the new environment not through interpreting the local language but also through creating a link on the cultural aspects to enable the spouse not to feel that much isolated. It consequently makes them feel that they are part of that community.

Spouses Cultural Program
The Spouse Cultural Program could essentially be considered an extension of the interpretation service owing to the fact that; the spouse here is taken through a thorough cultural training process. This training is aimed at enabling the spouse develops skills that are culturally appropriate as far as the host population is concerned. We understand how tough it is to be thrown into an absolutely new culture from the very beginning with the expectations to create your life right from scratch. It is the desire to make this transition for your spouse as easy as possible.

Recreational activities
The understanding of the fact that working all the time without other interests is counterproductive is the reason we have put in place this program. Our recreational program offers various fun-filled activities that help you and your spouse, new inhabitants of the host country, to learn more beyond what your roles at work are able to teach you. Some of these activities includes; cooking classes, dance workshops, Painting on canvas, Attending exciting events, art and crafts making, Swimming sessions, Athletic activities, Calligraphy Workshops, Yoga as well as Music classes.

Offspring Assistance

There is absolutely nothing that you can compare with the value of your children. It is for that matter that we have come up with various services that ensure that they have been to experience the very best upon relocating. Here are some of those services;

Language services
TE&OT’s on-site language services ensure that your kids are able to learn the host country’s language in a manner that is interactive with other children in their class, hence are also able to develop other essential social skills, as well as learn other cultural aspects of the people. There are various courses that are offered to kids in this program. Some of the areas that are covered in it include; Buds & Flowers which solely targets kids through pictures, songs, painting and coloring. The Future Talents course covers youth between 12-16 years and teaches all language levels. There are also Language Camps that are specifically designed for expat’s children and teenagers and are conducted in outdoor settings. The Standard Language Training on the other hand focuses on the main four skills of a language that entails Listening, Speaking, Reading and writing. Communication Skills is another class that is aimed at equipping the student with the essential tools in being good communicators. Finally, expats’ children are assisted to develop their listening, reading, writing and speaking skills in preparation for international exams based on what they have learnt in class.

School/university Search
There is nothing that is important as being able to place your child in the perfect institution of learning, no matter what academic level they are in. Our understanding of this very essential aspect when it comes to the education of your children has made us come up with the perfect manner through which we are able to help you, together with your young one select the right school that perfectly matches their academic needs.

Psychological care

Our Psychological care program is able to offer treatment services on a wide range of psychological problems. It is also important that we offer various avenues that this could be done, video correspondence included. Our highly trained, English speaking counselors are beyond compassionate to their patients, in a manner that has been proved to work perfectly.

Assistance with childcare/playgroup placements
We take every single right of a child very seriously and it is for that matter, we make it as simple as possible to help your child be able to enjoy these very fundamental rights. Through our Assistance with childcare/playgroup placements program, we are able to help your child interact with other children through play by organizing gatherings with other parents, helping them make new friends, hear native stories, enable them express their creativity, participate in arts and crafts /dances and learn about health, overall wellbeing and the culture of the natives.

Activities for Special needs children

We particularly care about the welfare of children with special needs, and it for this reason that we are determined that we have been able to provide every kid with special needs with the best personal care that is possible.
