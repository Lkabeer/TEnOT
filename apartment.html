How to move apartment?

Moving Hacks that will Make Your Life Easy

The organizing and packing of your valuables and most prized possessions can be exhausting. Things can become lost and broken over the period of a move. That’s why TE&OT has some tips for packing up your apartment and moving to a new destination.

  1. Moving clothes can be annoying. It seems repetitive to unfold everything and take them off of hangers, and then re-hang them at your new home. Instead tie multiple hangers together, keeping the clothes on them, and wrapping them in plastic. That way, when you get to your new home, unpacking your clothing and hanging them, becomes that much easier.
  2. Color-code your moving boxes with a corresponding “colored” room. This might mean that all boxes destined to be opened in the bedroom have a red sticker on the box. This helps you organize with the movers where you need what. It also cuts down on the unpacking time, by keeping all items organized.
  3. Pack your plates vertically; they are less likely to break that way. Place glasses with stems (wine glasses, for instance) upside down when packing. You can pack your stemware and glasses in socks.
  4. Instead of using loads of bubble-wrap, which rack up unnecessary costs, use your clothes to wrap fragile items. This will utilize all space, by packing your fragile items with clothes that need to be packed anyway.
  5. For small holes in the wall in the apartment you are moving out of, use a bar of soap. Rub the soap into the hole until it is filled. Save money on sealing holes or losing part of your deposit this way.
  6. Carry your important documents with you, whether they be financial, legal or identifying papers. Put them in envelopes and stick them in your personal bags/purses, so they don’t get lost or stolen.
  7. Make sure to pack an overnight bag filled with the essentials. That way you have the things you need the most, without having to look through boxes.
